Organization Governance Structure

The CBO has a management Board member drawn from different sectors of the community and relevant institutions with expertise and skills in various fields.

The role of the Board is to set policies for the CBO and oversee the organization.

The Board operates through sub-committees including Executive, Finance, Resource Mobilization and Project Outreach coordination.

Typical Membership Requirement

  • Identification documents (National ID or Passport)
  • Registration or membership fee: Ksh. 500
  • Proven common bond such as employment, business, or group affiliation

How to Support

Name Loan Fund (Ksh. 100,000)

Honor the people you care most about with a fund in their name or group. This fund offers circular interest-free loans to individuals, families and small business groups. Loans are repaid over a year period, recycling your donation to support more community members in future.

Areas of Focus (Any Amount)

Support specific sectors of the community that have been identified as most vulnerable by our economic experts. This allows donors to contribute meaningfully to social impact.

Impact Loans

Ogen leads the field of impact investments in community support. Instead of depositing your money in the bank, you can lend it to us as an impact deposit. These funds support loans to small businesses and non-profits while also providing a modest return.

Partnerships

Foundations, government bodies and corporate institutions partner with Ogen to expand outreach in our mission of financial inclusion. Collaboration with Ogen is an opportunity to improve communities through lasting social impact.

Rebuilding with Dignity

Inflation is rising, banks are tightening, and thousands of communities are left without a safety net. Your contribution becomes a lifeline, offering credit and guidance for families, businesses and nonprofits left behind by traditional financial institutions.

Impact of Every Ogen Loan

Every Ogen loan creates a ripple effect:

Businesses

Each loan supports 3–5 employees and their families, affecting up to 20 lives, and 5–10 local suppliers.

Families

Ogen loans reduce household debt by an average of 54%, changing entire family trajectories.

Nonprofits

Every loan enables nonprofits to impact around 500 people, amplifying community support.

Sustainable Impact

With ultra-low default rates of 0.65% for families, 0.5% for nonprofits, and 2.5% for businesses, loans are repaid with pride and reinvested to help the next borrower.
