Background Information

OGEN COMMUNITY CONNECTION is a Non-Governmental organization formed in 2025 to promote community development, youth empowerment and financial independence.

We support youth, women, small businesses and vulnerable groups through volunteerism, training, financial programs and community initiatives.

Vision

An empowered, healthy and financially free community.

Mission

To improve the quality of communities through sustainable development and financial empowerment initiatives.

Our Core Values

Strategic Lending

We support responsible and inclusive financial solutions that help individuals, families and community groups grow with dignity, stability and long-term opportunity.

Blended Finance

We combine partnerships, community support and sustainable funding approaches to expand access to opportunity and create deeper social and economic impact.

Financial Know-How

We equip our members and communities with practical financial knowledge, guidance and confidence to make informed decisions and build stronger livelihoods.

Our Objectives

  • Empower communities through microfinance and savings groups
  • Support income generating activities
  • Promote eco-tourism and environmental management
  • Build partnerships for development
  • Support vulnerable groups and youth development

Our Activities

  • Leadership & financial training programs
  • Youth empowerment (sports, theatre, mentorship)
  • Volunteer programs (local & international)
  • Peace and community training
  • Environmental and eco-tourism programs

Achievements

  • Distributed food to families during festive seasons
  • Trained over 500 youths in peace programs
  • Helped 100+ groups improve savings culture

Partnerships

We collaborate with government institutions, NGOs, and community groups to expand impact and improve livelihoods.

Products & Services

Group Savings (Chama)

Structured savings and credit facilities for community groups.

Business Loans

Financial support for small businesses and entrepreneurs.

Institution Support

Emergency and structured loans for employees and institutions.
